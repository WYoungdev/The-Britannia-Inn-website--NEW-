Our local area

Known as the "Gateway to Exmoor, Minehead is a beautiful place to explore all year round.

West Somerset Railway

Travel back in time on the West Somerset Railway, one of Britain's best
and longest heritage steam railways,
on a scenic journey through West Somerset.

Alcombe Combe

Old cottages line the route leading up through
Alcombe Combe to the open moor,
a walker's paradise and where mountain bikers
will find some challenging tracks.

Exmoor National Park

For the more energetic, we are
right on the doorstep of both the
Quantocks and Exmoor National Park.
Walk, cycle, ride - the choice is yours!
